Welcome to My Life Wall

My Life Wall is a place to gather the story of a life, one chapter at a time. Each brick on the wall is a chapter you can name yourself. Some chapters hold many memories, some are still waiting for theirs.

There is no right order and no deadline. Start with the chapters you remember most vividly and let the rest follow.

How to use this app

Click any chapter on the wall to open it, then write, speak, or add photos to record a memory in it. Use "Keep memory" to save it. Click the pencil on a brick to rename its chapter, or the star to mark it as the one you're currently working on. You can come back and edit or add more at any time.

Photos can be resized by dragging their corner handle, moved by dragging them, and given a caption by clicking just beneath them.

Use "Export backup" regularly to keep a copy of everything safe, and "Create Life Book" to turn the whole wall into a printable book.

You must configure a backup for your files. At the moment, this is not a cloud storage app, and you are responsible for backing up the data My Life Wall creates. Use the "Export backup" button regularly, this saves everything as one file you can store safely elsewhere. After clicking it, look in your Downloads folder for a file called "MyLifeWall Backup.json", that's the one to move somewhere safe. If this browser or device is ever lost, reset, or replaced, that exported file is the only way to bring your stories back.

Features in this app

The Home Wall — a brick for every chapter, coloured by whether it holds memories yet, and titled however you like. The memory editor — write, dictate, or photograph your way through a chapter. The Life Book — the whole wall gathered into a printable book. Backups — export and import everything as a single file.
