Welcome to My Life Wall

My Life Wall is a place to gather the story of a life, one year at a time. Each brick on the wall is a year. Some years hold many memories, some are still waiting for theirs.

There is no right order and no deadline. Start with the years you remember most vividly and let the rest follow.

How to use this app

Click any year on the wall to open it, then write, speak, or add photos to record a memory from that year. Use "Keep memory" to save it. You can come back and edit or add more at any time.

Photos can be resized by dragging their corner handle, moved by dragging them, and given a caption by clicking just beneath them.

Use "Export backup" regularly to keep a copy of everything safe, and "Create Life Book" to turn the whole wall into a printable book.

Features in this app

The Home Wall — a brick for every year of a life, coloured by whether it holds memories yet. The memory editor — write, dictate, or photograph your way through a year. The Life Book — the whole wall gathered into a printable book. Backups — export and import everything as a single file.
