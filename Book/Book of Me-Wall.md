# The Book of Me-Wall

## Purpose

The Book of Me-Wall is the complete living blueprint for the product.

It contains the philosophy, design principles, blueprints, architecture, specifications, and decisions required to build Me-Wall Genesis and future releases.

## Core Sentence

> Me-Wall is not a database of memories. It is a digital representation of a human life.

## Ultimate Design Ambition

> Every memory should leave future generations feeling as though they have just spent time with the person who lived it.
