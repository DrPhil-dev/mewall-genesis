# The Me-Wall Covenant

We recognise that every human life is unique, irreplaceable, and worthy of being remembered.

Every person who uses Me-Wall entrusts us with memories that cannot be recreated if lost. We therefore accept a responsibility that extends beyond software.

Our role is to preserve, connect, and honour the memories people choose to share, treating every memory with dignity, patience, and respect.

We are not preserving information. We are preserving the experience of being human. Technology is simply the quiet craft that makes that possible.
