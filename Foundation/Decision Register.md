# Decision Register

## AD-001: The individual is always the centre
Status: Accepted

Me-Wall is centred on one human life. All objects, memories, threads, media, and relationships radiate from the person.

## AD-002: There is no Save button
Status: Accepted

Memories should be preserved automatically. The user should never feel like they are managing files or performing computer tasks.

## AD-003: Stories are called Memories
Status: Accepted

“Memory” is warmer and less intimidating than “story.” Everyone has memories. Not everyone feels like a storyteller.

## AD-004: Threads, not Links
Status: Accepted

Related memories are woven into threads. “Threads” better reflects the human experience of life continuing across years.

## AD-005: The Home Wall contains no statistics
Status: Accepted

The value of a life must not be reduced to counts, totals, or productivity measures.

## AD-006: The project shall be governed by a Master Plan and Decision Register
Status: Accepted

All future design work shall be organised within the agreed governance structure before engineering begins.

## AD-007: The Living Memory Model is canonical
Status: Accepted

Every database, API, AI service, interface, and future platform shall derive from the Living Memory Model.

## AD-008: Meaning is part of the Living Memory Model
Status: Accepted

Meaning is not a separate blueprint. It is discovered through memories and relationships.
