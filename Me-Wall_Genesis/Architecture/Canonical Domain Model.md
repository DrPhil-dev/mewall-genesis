# Canonical Domain Model

## Person

The centre of Me-Wall.

A Person is not an account or a profile. A Person is revealed by memories.

## Account

The technical mechanism that permits access to Me-Wall.

An Account may authenticate a user, but it is not the person.

## Memory

The smallest meaningful unit of remembered life.

## Thread

A natural continuity across memories.

Examples:

- Nicole Thread
- BlueKee Thread
- Swimming Thread
- Mathematics Thread
- Father Thread

## Media

Evidence supporting memory.

Examples:

- photograph
- video
- audio
- transcript
- letter
- certificate
- email
- document

Media is evidence. It is not the centre.
