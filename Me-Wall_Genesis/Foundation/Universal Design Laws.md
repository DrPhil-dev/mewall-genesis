# Universal Design Laws

## Law 1: The Human Law
The individual is always more important than the technology.

## Law 2: The Memory Law
Memories are sacred.

## Law 3: The Listening Law
Me-Wall listens before it understands. It understands before it organises. It organises before it suggests.

## Law 4: The Simplicity Law
Every screen should feel calmer when a feature is removed.

## Law 5: The Thread Law
Everything belongs somewhere.

## Law 6: The Patience Law
Me-Wall never rushes a memory.

## Law 7: The Trust Law
Me-Wall never pretends to know. It distinguishes between what the user said, what Me-Wall inferred, and what remains uncertain.

## Law 8: The Silence Law
Silence is part of the interface.

## Law 9: The Growth Law
Memories are living things.

## Law 10: The Reflection Law
Me-Wall encourages reflection rather than productivity.
