# Functional Specification 001: Home Wall

## HW-001
The Home Wall shall display one brick for every year from the person’s birth year to a configurable future horizon. Default horizon: 100 years.

## HW-002
Every brick shall have identical dimensions. No year shall be visually larger or smaller than another.

## HW-003
The current year shall be subtly distinguished using light, not dramatic colour or animation.

## HW-004
Future years shall remain visible to represent possibility rather than absence.

## HW-005
Selecting a brick shall transition into the corresponding year view using a calm zoom or focus transition.

Maximum transition time: 500 milliseconds.

## HW-006
No statistics shall appear on the Home Wall.

## HW-007
The Home Wall shall remain usable without reading instructions.

## HW-008
The Home Wall shall support mouse, touch, keyboard, and screen readers.

## HW-009
The Home Wall shall never display advertising, notifications, badges, gamification, streaks, leaderboards, or progress bars.

## HW-010
The Home Wall shall preserve visual calm. If an interface element does not directly support memory, it shall not appear.
